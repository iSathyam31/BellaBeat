This is the FitBit Fitness Tracker Data data which is stored on Kaggle click here

This data set contains multiple CSV files which gives us useful information about the 30 users. The data also follows the ROCCC approach in some extent. Reliability , Original , Comprehensive , Current , Cited

Reliability : The data is from 30 FitBit users who consented to the submission of personal tracker data and generated by from a distributed survey

Original : This data set generated by respondents to a distributed survey via Amazon Mechanical Turk between 12.03.2016-12.05.2016.

Comprehensive : Thirty eligible Fitbit users consented to the submission of personal tracker data, including minute-level output for physical activity, heart rate, and sleep monitoring.

Current : Data is from March 2016 to May 2016. So the data is not current which means the users habit may be different now

Cited : The third-party data set was available by Mobius via Kaggle

However , the data set has limitations

  • Since the data is of 30 users only , a large scale analysis cannot be made
  • For the 8 user data for weight, 5 users manually entered their weight and 3 recorded via a connected WiFi device (eg: WiFi scale).
  • Most data is recorded from Tuesday to Thursday, which may not be comprehensive enough to form an accurate analysis.

Useful Insights

  • Hourly step count better represents being active than daily step count
  • The less active one is, the less sleep one gets
  • People tend to spend more time in bed sleeping and awake on Sundays and they spend most time doing physical activities on Saturdays
  • Non active person is likely to have a High BMI

Recommendations

  • Bellabeat can include function in Bellabeat app to alert user who tends to have a high number to sedentary minutes

  • Bellabeat can include timely notification in Leaf/Time to motivate user to move around regularly to reduce their sedentary minutes

  • The app should also encourage users to have time-outs for meditations and stress management routine like breathing exercises, yoga and mindful meditations.

  • Finally, the app should encourage users to meet the recommended 10,000 steps and at least 7 hours of sleep daily
